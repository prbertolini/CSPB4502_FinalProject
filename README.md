Determining Best Predictors of F1 Race REsults in Hybrid Era

This project aims to take race data and determine which factors in a race have the most significant impact on the race results.

1. What has the greatest impact on race results?
   A. Qualifying with constructors also playing a role.
2. How do constructors impact the race?
   A: They give a clear underlying advantage that improves the performance in qualifying and thus the race.
